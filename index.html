Welcome to MESA

We are Mighty Eagles Soccer Academy /F.C
We are located at JKUAT grounds near Muthoni market in Kitale, Transnzoia county

Who are we??

Wea are a soccer academy located in Kitale, Transnzoia County at JKUAT Kitale Campus grounds, formerly Robinsons Academy. We are dedicated to developing young soccer players into skilled and confident athletes. Our soccer academy offers a comprehensive training program that is designed to provide your child with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed on the field. We have a team of experienced coaches who are committed to helping your child reach their full potential as a soccer player.

About us

Our Core Values

Passion for the Game:

At our soccer club, we believe in fostering a deep and unwavering passion for the beautiful game. This value emphasizes the love and enthusiasm for soccer that extends from players to staff, and from supporters to sponsors. We strive to create an environment where everyone associated with the club is driven by a genuine love for the sport, creating a positive and vibrant atmosphere that resonates both on and off the field.

Unity and Teamwork:

Teamwork is the foundation of success in soccer, and our club is committed to promoting unity both on and off the pitch. We value collaboration, respect, and open communication among players, coaching staff, and the entire organization. This sense of unity extends beyond the club to include our fans and community, creating a powerful sense of belonging that strengthens our collective spirit.

Integrity and Fair Play:

Upholding the highest standards of integrity and fair play is non-negotiable at our soccer club. We are committed to competing with honesty, respecting rules and regulations, and maintaining transparency in all our dealings. This value ensures that the club's reputation remains untarnished, fostering trust among supporters, sponsors, and the broader soccer community.

Continuous Improvement:

In the dynamic world of soccer, constant improvement is essential for long-term success. Our club values a growth mindset and a commitment to continuous improvement at all levels. This extends to player development, coaching techniques, organizational processes, and community engagement. By embracing innovation and learning from every experience, we aim to stay at the forefront of the evolving soccer landscape.

Services

At MESA, we believe that proper training is essential for young footballers. Not only does it help them improve their skills and technique, but it also ensures that they stay safe on the field. Football is a physically demanding sport, and young players are more susceptible to injuries if they don't receive proper training. We as the Eagles believe that football is a sport that can benefit children in many ways.

Youth Development Program:

Our Youth Development Program is designed to nurture and develop young soccer talents, providing comprehensive training for children aged 6 to 16. The program focuses on technical skills, tactical awareness, physical conditioning, and character development. Experienced coaches use age-appropriate training methodologies to ensure that each participant reaches their full potential. Regular assessments and feedback sessions are conducted to monitor progress and tailor the training to individual needs.

Fitness and Conditioning Workshops:

Enhance the performance of players with our specialized fitness and conditioning workshops. Tailored for different age groups and skill levels, these workshops focus on improving agility, strength, speed, and endurance. Certified fitness trainers work closely with players to create personalized training plans, incorporating soccer-specific exercises to enhance overall athletic performance and reduce the risk of injuries.

Community Outreach Clinics:

Our soccer club is committed to engaging with the local community through free or low-cost soccer clinics. These clinics serve as an opportunity to introduce the sport to youngsters, promote physical activity, and instill a sense of teamwork and discipline. Experienced coaches organize fun and interactive sessions suitable for various age groups, fostering a love for soccer and promoting a healthy lifestyle.

College Recruitment Assistance:

At : Aspiring young players often dream of playing soccer at the collegiate level. Our club provides a comprehensive college recruitment assistance program to guide players through the process. This includes preparing player profiles, creating highlight reels, organizing showcase events, and connecting players with college scouts. The goal is to help talented players secure opportunities to play soccer at the collegiate level while pursuing their academic goals. Our network of contacts within the soccer community and colleges enhances the chances of successful recruitment for our players.
